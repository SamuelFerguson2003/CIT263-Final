About the Artist

Sarah Ferguson is an artist and art instructor from Philippi, WV where she lives with her husband, Pete, and son, Samuel. She has an Art Education degree from West Liberty State College, and has taught art for many years, in many different capacities. Sarah currently teaches a weekly adult watercolor Zoom class, private in-home art lessons, workshops at the Randolph County Arts Center in Elkins, WV, and more.
